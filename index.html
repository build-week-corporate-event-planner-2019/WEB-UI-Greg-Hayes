About Us

Who We Are

The Event Planner team is a group of talented developers who have taken on the task of building an innovative tool for creating and managing corporate events of any size, scale, or scope.

What We Do

We have developed an event management and workflow tool designed for the challenges facing corporate event planners. Event Planner lets you access events through your account and make changes on the fly.

Features

Manage your events easily using our system. See all of your upcoming events, with the ability to update or delete any details. Create new events with our user-friendly guided form to make sure your next event is a success!
